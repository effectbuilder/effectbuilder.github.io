Everything in one place

RGBJunkie is made for people who want their setup to look intentional—whether you are fine-tuning a stream backdrop or finally lining up the strips behind your monitor.

See your layout before you stick anything

Arrange keyboards, strips, and accessories on a large workspace. Move, resize, rotate, and align pieces until the picture matches the desk you want.

Connect the hardware you already own

Add supported USB lighting devices, keep them organized in a simple list, and use familiar actions like show or hide and quick “find my device” flashes.

Browse and tune gorgeous effects

Pick from bundled looks, adjust sliders to taste, and keep your favorite moods separate from your physical layout so swapping styles stays effortless.

Fine control when one LED matters

Open LED Studio from Settings to paint specific lights by hand—perfect for accents, logos, or cleaning up a single stubborn zone.

Let your music move the mood

When you want the room to pulse with your playlist or game audio, optional music-driven lighting helps the colors follow the energy in the room.

Save scenes and come back anytime

Store whole setups—layout, effect choices, and custom LED touches—so weekday focus mode and weekend party mode are one click apart.

New apps sometimes show a Windows SmartScreen notice. Choose “More info” and then “Run anyway” if you trust this download from RGBJunkie. Digitally signed installers reduce these prompts over time.

What you need

  • Computer: Windows 10 or newer, 64-bit, desktop or laptop.
  • Lighting: USB RGB devices that RGBJunkie supports out of the box. Browse supported USB devices and layout parts.
  • Screen: At least a 1200×800 window size is comfortable for the full workspace.
