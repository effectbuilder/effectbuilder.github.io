Help & Quick Guide

Welcome to the RGBJunkie Component Builder. Here are the tools available in your new interface.

New: Clear the canvas for a fresh project.
Save: Sync your component to the cloud (Login required).
Share: Generate a public link for your component.
Import: Load a .json file from your computer.
Export: Download the final file for SignalRGB or WLED.
Gallery: Browse and fork components created by the community.

  • Select (V): Select, move, and drag-box LEDs.
  • Place (P): Click to drop LEDs and auto-wire them.
  • Wiring (W): Manually connect or break LED chains.
  • Image Tool (I): Move and scale your background reference guide.
  • Rotate: Turn selection 90°. (Shift+Click for clockwise).
  • Scale (S): Resize selected LEDs from their center.
  • Mirror (M): Flip selection horizontally or vertically.

We've reorganized the controls to give you more room to work:

  • Properties Tab: Edit the device name, brand, and type. This is also where you upload your device image.
  • Build Tab: Find all the Quick-Add shapes (Matrix, Circle, Strips, etc.) to start your design faster.
About RGBJunkie Component Builder

RGBJunkie Component Builder is a web-based visual tool designed to simplify the creation of custom component .json files for the SignalRGB platform.

Built by José A. Miranda Vélez in the beautiful island of Puerto Rico, this project aims to provide an intuitive, grid-based interface for designers and enthusiasts to map out LEDs for their custom devices.
