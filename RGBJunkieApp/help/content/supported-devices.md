## Is my gear supported?

We add USB and network devices over time. The live list lives here:

**[Supported gear](/RGBJunkieApp/supported/)**

Worth a look before you buy — or when something doesn’t show up after **Rescan**.

## What usually just works

* **USB** controllers we ship plugins for
* **WLED** on your LAN after you add the IP — [WLED setup](wled-setup)
* **RAM and graphics-card RGB** built into the app on Windows — [RAM and GPU lighting](ram-gpu-lighting)

Some gear still needs a **layout** from the [RGB Wizard](rgb-wizard) or [Component Library](component-library) before anything lights up. Detection ≠ instant rainbow.

## Device missing from the tree?

1. Plug in **directly** — skip the sketchy hub for the first test
2. **Rescan** in the [Devices panel](devices-panel)
3. Community **plugin** from [Installed files](installed-files) if the supported list points at one
4. Quit other RGB apps hogging the same controller

Still nothing? [Troubleshooting — no lights](troubleshooting-no-lights) and [Send a support report](send-support-report).

## Virtual / plugin devices

Plugins can add **virtual** rows — wallpaper capture, screen sync, that sort of thing. Treat them like hardware: assign components, pick an effect, save a [Scene](scene-profiles).
